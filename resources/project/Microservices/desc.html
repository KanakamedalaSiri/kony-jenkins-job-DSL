Guide

This is the home of your App FactoryTM Microservice project.
It's defined to contain a collection of jobs and credentials to help you configure your microservice.

  • The jobs are predefined and you only need to worry about providing the input parameters you'd like them to use.
  • The credentials are for you to define and will provide your jobs with the access they'll need to integrate with other tools on your behalf.

Jobs

Every App FactoryTM Microservice project comes with the following jobs:

  • Configuring your Microservice
  • Define Watchers and Schedulers for building periodically or whenever changes are made to your project.

Credentials

Every App FactoryTM Microservice project will require you to define credentials of the following types:

  • SCM User & Password or User & Personal Access Token
